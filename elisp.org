* Local Variables(let)
- https://www.gnu.org/software/emacs/manual/html_node/elisp/Local-Variables.html
 
#+BEGIN_SRC emacs-lisp
  (setq y 2)
  ⇒ 2

  (let ((y 1)
        (z y)) ; 'z' is bound to the old value of 'y'
    (list y z))
  ⇒ (1 2)

  (let* ((y 1)
         (z y)) ; Use the just-established value of y.
    (list y z))
  ⇒ (1 1)
#+END_SRC
